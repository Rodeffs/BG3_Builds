SOME PERMA BUFFS USEFUL FOR ANY BUILD:
* Act 1: Get Auntie Ethel to low HP and then spare her to get +1 to any Ability (one-time use)
* Act 2: In the Moonrise Towers speak to Araj Oblodra with Astarion in party and make him drink her blood to get Potion of Everlasting Vigour for +2 STR (one-time use)
* Act 3: At the Mirror of Loss in the House of Grief you may get +1 CHR. Also, if you pass DC 25 Religion check, then you may get +2 to any Ability (once per character)

Swashbuckler Rogue 12

* Starting Abilities: 8 STR, 15+2 DEX, 15+1 CON, 10 INT, 13 WIS, 10 CHR
* Feat order: Sharpshooter -> Ability Improvement +2 DEX  or Athlete +1 DEX -> Crossbow Expert -> Lucky, Ability Improvement +2 DEX or anything else
* TIP: In Act 3 get Bhaalist Armour
* TIP: Use two Hand Crossbows to maximize Sharpshooter
* The idea is to get close to an enemy and then shoot them with a crossbow. This will provide: bonus dmg from Sharpshooter, no disadvantage due to Crossbow Expert, Sneak Attack due to Swashbuckler and bonus from Bhaalist Armour. At level 12 with two Hand Crossbows +2, Bhaalist Armour bonus and 22 DEX you will deal 76~156 dmg per turn

Arcane Trickster Rogue 12

* Starting Abilities: 8 STR, 15+2 DEX, 15+1 CON, 14 INT, 10 WIS, 8 CHR
* Feat order: Sharpshooter -> Weapon Master +1 DEX -> Athlete +1 DEX or Ability Improvement +2 DEX -> Ability Increase +2 CON, Lucky, or anything else
* General recommendations:
    * Weapon Master is needed to get proficiency with Heavy Crossbows and Longbows
    * In Act 2 get Eversight Ring
    * In Act 3 get Bhaalist Armour
    * Cast Fog Cloud or Darkness, then hide in it. Every turn step out of it, shoot then hide back in. With Eversight Ring however, you may shoot enemies while being inside of it
    * Race - Lightfoot Halfling, Duergar, Half-Elf, Elf
    * Cantrips - Friends (for the main character), Minor Illusion
    * Good Level 1 Spells: Fog Cloud, Shield, Tasha's Hideous Laughter, Magic Missile, Mage Armour (if DEX < 20 and you have The Graceful Cloth), Feather Fall, Longstrider, Enhance Leap
    * Good Level 2 Spells: Darkness, Misty Step, Invisibility, Hold Person, Mirror Image, Blur
